# Bluetooth Features

We use all features from [RAPIDS-Bluetooth](https://www.rapids.science/1.6/features/phone-bluetooth/), 
including the number of scans of participants' own devices and others' devices, as well as the unique count of these devices.

<br/>

    <tr>
        <td><strong>Feature Name</strong></td>
        <td><strong>Unit</strong></td>
        <td><strong>Description</strong></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`countscans`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Number of scans (rows) from the devices sensed during a time segment instance. The more scans a bluetooth device has the longer it remained within range of the participantâ€™s phone.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`uniquedevices`</td>
        <td>devices</td>
        <td>Number of unique bluetooth devices sensed during a time segment instance as identified by their hardware addresses.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`meanscans`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Mean of the scans of every sensed device within each time segment instance.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`stdscans`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Standard deviation of the scans of every sensed device within each time segment instance.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`countscansmostfrequentdevicewithinsegments`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Number of scans of the most sensed device within each time segment instance.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`countscansleastfrequentdevicewithinsegments`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Number of scans of the least sensed device within each time segment instance.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`countscansmostfrequentdeviceacrosssegments`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Number of scans of the most sensed device across time segment instances of the same type.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`countscansleastfrequentdeviceacrosssegments`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Number of scans of the least sensed device across time segment instances of the same type per device.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`countscansmostfrequentdeviceacrossdataset`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Number of scans of the most sensed device across the entire dataset of every participant.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>`countscansleastfrequentdeviceacrossdataset`</td>
        <td>scans</td>
        <td>Number of scans of the least sensed device across the entire dataset of every participant.</td>
    </tr>
